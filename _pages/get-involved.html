Tech4Good Bristol is completely non-funded and is run by a small group of volunteers to set up meet-up events that are focused on bringing tech expertise and know-how to the not-for-profit sector so that we can work together for the social good.

If you are able to help out in some way, we'd love to hear from you! Some areas where help would be useful include:

  • Ideas for future meet-up topics
  • Help with the administration of the group
  • Help at events to sign people in at the door and generally help out at particular events
  • Authoring of guest posts on the blog
